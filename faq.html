🛠 Repair Services

Yes! All our repairs come with a minimum 30-day warranty on parts and labor. For major repairs involving compressors, motors, or PCBs, we offer extended warranty options of up to 6 months. The warranty covers the same issue recurring - if the problem returns within the warranty period, we fix it free of charge.
We offer same-day service for most areas within Anand. For urgent issues like a non-functioning refrigerator or AC during peak summer, we prioritize getting to you as quickly as possible - often within 2-4 hours. For non-urgent repairs, we can schedule a visit at your convenience, including weekends.
We service all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Godrej, Haier, Voltas, Daikin, Hitachi, Bosch, IFB, Panasonic, Blue Star, Carrier, and many more. Our technicians are trained to handle both domestic and international brands, including older models that other service centers might not support.
Repair costs vary depending on the appliance type, the problem, and parts needed. Simple fixes may cost a few hundred rupees, while major repairs involving compressor or motor replacement can be more significant. We always provide a detailed quote before starting work - you'll know the exact cost upfront with no hidden charges. We never proceed without your approval.
Yes, we prioritize genuine OEM parts whenever available. For older models where original parts may be discontinued, we use high-quality compatible parts that meet or exceed original specifications. We never use cheap knockoffs that could damage your appliance or fail prematurely. All parts come with their own warranty.

🛒 Buying Used Appliances

Absolutely. Every used appliance goes through a rigorous 25-point inspection before we list it for sale. This includes checking all components, testing functionality under various conditions, and ensuring safety standards are met. Appliances that don't pass our quality standards are either refurbished or responsibly recycled - we never sell anything we wouldn't use ourselves.
All our used appliances come with warranty coverage ranging from 3 to 6 months, depending on the type and age of the appliance. Refrigerators and washing machines typically get 6 months, while smaller appliances get 3 months. The warranty covers any functional defects - if something goes wrong, we repair or replace it at no cost to you.
Customers typically save 40-60% compared to buying new. For example, a good quality used refrigerator that would cost 25,000-30,000 rupees new might be available for 12,000-15,000 from us in excellent condition with warranty. The exact savings depend on the brand, model, age, and condition of the specific appliance.
Yes! We provide free delivery within Anand for all appliances purchased from us. Our team also helps with basic installation - placing the appliance, connecting it, and ensuring it's working properly before we leave. For AC installation requiring mounting and electrical work, a nominal installation charge may apply.

💵 Selling Your Appliance

We buy appliances in any condition - working or not working. Of course, working appliances in good condition fetch better prices. But even if your appliance has issues, we'll make you an offer. Non-repairable units are responsibly recycled, and you still get paid for the scrap value. Don't assume your old appliance is worthless - contact us for a quote!
We consider several factors: brand reputation, model, age, current condition, market demand, and any repair needs. Premium brands and newer models command higher prices. We research current market values to ensure our offers are fair. You can always decline our offer with no obligation - we never pressure anyone to sell.
Yes, pickup is completely free within our service area in Anand. Our team comes to your location with a vehicle, handles all the heavy lifting, and takes the appliance away. You don't have to move it or prepare anything special. Just make sure we have clear access to the appliance on pickup day.
You get paid immediately at the time of pickup. We offer multiple payment options: cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), or direct bank transfer. Just let us know your preference when scheduling the pickup. No waiting for checks to clear or pending payments - you get your money on the spot.
