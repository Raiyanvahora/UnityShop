Oven & Microwave Repair

Don't let a broken microwave or oven disrupt your kitchen routine. Our experts repair all types of cooking appliances quickly and affordably.

  • Microwave not heating issues
  • Magnetron and capacitor replacement
  • Turntable motor repair
  • OTG heating element replacement
  • Thermostat and timer fixes
  • Door switch and latch repair

We service solo microwaves, convection microwaves, OTGs, and built-in ovens from all major brands.
