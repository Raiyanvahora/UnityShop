1. Introduction

Welcome to Unity Appliances. These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our website and services. By accessing our website or purchasing from us, you agree to be bound by these terms. Please read them carefully before proceeding with any transaction.

2. Products and Services

Unity Appliances specializes in:

  • Selling used and refurbished home appliances (refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines, ovens)
  • Purchasing old or damaged appliances from customers
  • Repair and servicing of home appliances
  • Selling genuine spare parts for various appliances

All products listed are subject to availability. We reserve the right to limit quantities and refuse orders at our discretion.

3. Product Condition

Our products are categorized as follows:

  • Refurbished: Appliances that have been professionally restored to working condition, cleaned, and tested. May show minor cosmetic wear.
  • Used: Pre-owned appliances in good working condition with normal wear and tear.
  • Sale Items: Products offered at discounted prices due to overstock or minor cosmetic imperfections.
  • New Parts: Brand new genuine or compatible spare parts.

4. Pricing and Payment

All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (INR) and are subject to change without prior notice. We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Bank Transfer / NEFT / RTGS
  • Debit / Credit Cards

Full payment is required before or at the time of delivery unless otherwise agreed upon.

5. Delivery and Installation

Delivery charges may apply based on your location and the size of the appliance. Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to circumstances beyond our control.

  • Basic delivery includes bringing the appliance to your doorstep.
  • Installation services are available at additional cost for certain appliances.
  • Please inspect the appliance upon delivery before signing the receipt.

6. Warranty Terms

Warranty coverage varies by product:

  • Refurbished appliances: 3 to 6 months warranty on parts and labor
  • Used appliances: 3 months warranty on functional components
  • Spare parts: 30 days replacement warranty for manufacturing defects

Warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by misuse, neglect, or accidents
  • Cosmetic damage or normal wear and tear
  • Damage from power surges or improper voltage
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Consumable parts (filters, bulbs, etc.)
