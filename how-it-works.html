Our Process

Simple, Transparent, Hassle-Free

Whether you need a repair, want to buy a quality used appliance, or want to sell your old one, our process is designed to be straightforward and convenient.

🛠

Book a Repair

Get your appliance fixed quickly with our streamlined repair process.

1
📞

Contact Us

Reach out via phone, WhatsApp, or our contact form. Describe the issue with your appliance - the brand, model, and what's going wrong.

2
📅

Schedule a Visit

We'll find a convenient time slot for you. Same-day service is available for most issues. Our technician will arrive at your doorstep as scheduled.

3
🔍

Diagnosis & Quote

Our technician thoroughly examines your appliance and identifies the problem. You receive an upfront quote with no hidden charges before any work begins.

4
🛠

Expert Repair

Upon your approval, we fix the issue using genuine parts. Most repairs are completed on the same visit. Complex issues may require a follow-up.

5
✅

Quality Check & Warranty

We test the appliance thoroughly before leaving. You receive a warranty card for the repair, giving you peace of mind.

🛒

Buy a Used Appliance

Get quality pre-owned appliances with warranty at unbeatable prices.

1
💬

Tell Us What You Need

Contact us with your requirements - type of appliance, preferred brands, capacity needs, and budget. We'll check our current inventory.

2
📸

View Available Options

We share photos and details of matching appliances from our stock. You can also visit our workshop to see them in person if you prefer.

3
💰

Confirm & Pay

Once you've selected your appliance, confirm your order. We accept cash, UPI, and bank transfers. Pay the full amount or a booking advance.

4
🚚

Free Delivery & Setup

We deliver to your home at a scheduled time and help with basic installation. Our team ensures everything is working perfectly before they leave.

5
📋

Warranty Activated

You receive a warranty card (3-6 months depending on appliance) covering any defects. Enjoy your new-to-you appliance with confidence!

Sell Your Old Appliance

Turn your unused appliances into instant cash with our hassle-free process.

1
📸

Share Details & Photos

Send us photos of your appliance along with basic details - brand, model, age, and condition. You can do this via WhatsApp or our contact form.

2
💰

Get an Instant Quote

Based on your information, we provide an estimated price. This is a fair market value considering the appliance's age, brand, and condition.

3
🚚

Free Pickup Scheduled

If you accept the quote, we schedule a free pickup from your location. Our team handles all the heavy lifting - you don't have to do a thing.

4
🔍

Quick Inspection

Our team does a quick inspection at pickup to verify the condition matches your description. The final price is confirmed on the spot.

5
💲

Instant Payment

Get paid immediately - cash, UPI, or bank transfer. No waiting, no hassle. Your old appliance is now someone else's treasure!
