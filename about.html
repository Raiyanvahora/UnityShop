Welcome to Unity Appliances

Unity Appliances was founded with a simple mission: to make quality home appliances accessible and affordable for everyone. We specialize in buying, selling, and repairing used and refurbished home appliances including refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines, and ovens.

Our team of expert technicians thoroughly inspects, services, and tests every appliance before it reaches your home. We believe in sustainability and giving appliances a second life, reducing electronic waste while helping you save money.

With over 8 years of experience in the industry, we have served thousands of happy customers across the region. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart from the competition.
