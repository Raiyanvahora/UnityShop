Refund & Returns Policy

Home / Refund & Returns Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

At Unity Appliances, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We understand that buying used or refurbished appliances can be a concern, which is why we have a clear and fair return and refund policy.

7-Day Return Policy

We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery for all appliances purchased from Unity Appliances. Within this period, you may return the product if:

  • The appliance does not function as described at the time of purchase
  • There is a significant defect that was not disclosed before sale
  • The product delivered is different from what was ordered
  • There is damage that occurred during delivery (must be reported within 24 hours)

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The appliance must be in the same condition as when it was delivered
  • All original accessories, manuals, and packaging (if provided) must be returned
  • The appliance must not show signs of misuse, damage, or unauthorized repair attempts
  • You must have proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
  • Return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Spare parts once installed
  • Products with physical damage caused after delivery
  • Appliances that have been modified or repaired by unauthorized persons
  • Products sold "as-is" or "final sale" (clearly marked at time of purchase)
  • Items returned after the 7-day window

How to Initiate a Return

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Call us at +91 98765 43210 or email info@unityappliances.com within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide Details: Share your order number, reason for return, and photos/videos if applicable.
  3. Inspection: Our team may visit to inspect the appliance to verify the issue.
  4. Approval: Once approved, we will arrange for pickup of the appliance.
  5. Processing: Refund or exchange will be processed after the returned item is received and inspected.

Refund Options

Upon approval of your return, you may choose from the following options:

  • Full Refund: Receive a full refund to your original payment method
  • Exchange: Exchange for another product of equal or greater value (pay the difference if applicable)
  • Store Credit: Receive store credit for future purchases

Refund Processing Time

Refunds are processed within the following timeframes after the returned product is received and inspected:

  • Cash payments: Refund within 3-5 business days via bank transfer or UPI
  • UPI payments: Refund within 3-5 business days to the original UPI ID
  • Card payments: Refund within 7-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Bank transfers: Refund within 5-7 business days

Delivery Charges

Regarding delivery charges for returns:

  • If the return is due to a defect or our error: Full delivery charges will be refunded
  • If the return is due to change of mind: Original delivery charges are non-refundable
  • Return pickup: Free pickup is provided for all eligible returns

Warranty vs. Return Policy

Please note the difference between our return policy and warranty:

  • Return Policy: Covers issues discovered within 7 days of delivery. Results in refund or exchange.
  • Warranty: Covers manufacturing defects and failures occurring after 7 days but within the warranty period (3-6 months). Results in free repair or replacement of defective parts.

If you experience issues after 7 days but within your warranty period, please contact us for warranty service instead of a return.

Damaged During Delivery

If your appliance arrives damaged:

  1. Note the damage on the delivery receipt before signing
  2. Take photos/videos of the damage immediately
  3. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  4. We will arrange for replacement or full refund at no extra cost

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for products of similar or higher value. If the replacement item costs more, you will need to pay the difference. If it costs less, the difference will be refunded.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your order:

  • Before dispatch: Full refund with no deductions
  • After dispatch but before delivery: Full refund minus shipping costs incurred
  • After delivery: Subject to our return policy above
